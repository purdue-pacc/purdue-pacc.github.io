Our 4-Step Process

  1. Join the Discord.
    Click this invite link: Join our Discord server
  2. Type !verify.
    Once you’re in the server, go to the appropriate channel and type !verify to start the verification process.
  3. Provide your Purdue email to the bot.
    Follow the instructions from the bot and submit your official Purdue email so we can verify you as a member.
  4. Show up at the workouts!
    After you’re verified, check the workout calendar and just show up. We’ll take care of the rest.
