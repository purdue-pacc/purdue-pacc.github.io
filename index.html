PACC

A club focused on building strength, activity, and community for Purdue students.

The Parkour and Calisthenics Club is a community of Purdue students who train together. Our mission is to provide an active and encouraging community for those who want to strengthen their minds and bodies through parkour and calisthenics. This club hopes to inspire and motivate those who are interested in discovering a unique form of exercise. We meet 6 days a week and accommodate all skill levels, from beginners to experts.

Practice days focus on either parkour or calisthenics. We host parkour days 3 times a week, which focus on perfecting new moves such as vaults and rolls, incorporating our surroundings to help us flow through the environment, and preparing for safety and evacuation scenarios. For those who are advanced, we’ll focus on applying our skills in the real world and performing more complex flows. Calisthenics days are held 3 times a week and focus on strengthening specific muscle groups through targeted exercise. We host upper-body, lower-body, core, and skill-based workouts. Be prepared to push yourself past your limits!

How to join

  • Type !verify.
    Once you're in the server, go to #bot-commands and type !verify to start the verification process.
  • Provide your Purdue email to the bot.
    Follow the instructions from the bot and submit your official Purdue email so we can verify you as a member.
  • Show up at the workouts!
    After you're verified, check the workout calendar and just show up!

Prior Collabs

We're open to joint events, workshops, training sessions, and creative collabs that support students, fitness, and community-building!

To learn more, please contact us through either discord or instagram!

Guest Coach event

During the fall we have Scotty from F8 Movement come to West Lafayette and do some workouts with his equipment and team! Really good for people who want to test their limits with speed and endurance.

PACC x BJJ

In fall 2025, PACC did a collab with the Brazillian Ju Jitsu club, learning combat skills and how to safely break out of holds!

PACC x CHAARG

In Fall 2025, we did a collab with CHAARG, or Changing Health Attitudes + Actions to Recreate Girls, combining parkour and Calisthenics to give a boost to their exercise routines!

American Ninja Warrior Gym

Every so often, the club will go to Parkour gyms and American Ninja Warrior Gyms in Indianapolis! Lots of fun, and great parkour practice!
